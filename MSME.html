MSME Registration

MSMEs are the backbone of the Indian economy, accounting for approximately 50% of total exports, 45% of manufacturing sector output, and approximately 95% of total industrial units. MSMEs are typically divided into two categories: manufacturing and services, and they have significant growth potential in the Indian economy. Regardless of the type of business you own or plan to start, the first step should be MSME registration. This is primarily to take advantage of MSME registration benefits such as tax breaks, government service discounts, and simplified borrowing terms that the government offers to this critical sector.

Getting affordable loans is pivotal to the early success and growth of any MSME. This is possible when you avail of the Bajaj Finserv MSME Loan, as it can help you meet market demands with ease. Upscale production by investing in newer machinery or improving your infrastructure to meet growing demand.

MSME registration assists micro, small, and medium (MSME) industries in obtaining various government benefits for growth. MSME industries are the backbone of the economy in a developing country like India. When these industries grow, so does the country’s economy. These are also known as small-scale industries (SSIs).

The Government of India has not yet made MSME registration mandatory, but it is beneficial to get one’s business registered under this because it provides many benefits in terms of taxation, business setup, credit facilities, loans, and so on.
